Introduction

A sport car is a vehicle designed for speed, agility and performance. These cars typically have powerful engines, advanced suspension systems and aerodynamic designs. Sport cars are built for those who love the thrill of driving and enjoy the feel of a high-performance vehicle on the road.

Types

Sports cars

Sports cars are designed for performance and handling. They typically have a low profile, two seats, and a powerful engine. They are ideal for driving on winding roads and offer an exciting driving experience.

Muscle cars

Muscle cars are typically larger and heavier than sports cars. They are designed for straight-line speed and acceleration and have powerful engines. They are popular for drag racing and car shows.

Exotic cars

Exotic cars are high-performance vehicles that are often hand-built and feature advanced technology and materials. They are designed to stand out and offer a unique driving experience.
